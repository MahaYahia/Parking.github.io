PARKING SYSTEM
'PROTOTYPE'

The Parking Assistance Prototype is designed to enhance vehicle parking safety by utilizing ultrasonic sensors. These sensors are strategically placed at different angles around the parking area to detect the presence of a car or object. The system continuously measures the distance between the vehicle and the wall, providing real-time feedback to the driver.

If the distance between the car and the wall is less than 30 cm, a warning message is displayed on the screen, and a buzzer sounds to alert the driver. If no object is detected, the system indicates that the area is clear, ensuring that parking is safe and free from obstacles. This prototype combines simple yet effective technology to improve the parking experience by reducing the risk of collisions.
